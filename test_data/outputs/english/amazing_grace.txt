Uh-may-zing grays! How sweet thuh sownd!
That sayvd uh rech lyke mee!
Eye wuhns wuz lawst, but now am fownd,
Wuz blynd, but now Eye sea.

'Twuz grays that tawt mai hart tuh feer,
And grays mai feerz ree-leevd;
How presh-us did that grays uh-peer
Thuh ow-er Eye furst bee-leevd!

Throo men-ee dayn-jurz, toylz, and snehrz,
Eye hav awl-red-ee kuhm;
'Tiz grays haz brawt mee sayf thus fahr,
And grays wil leed mee hohm.
